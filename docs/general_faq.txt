SmartHeat Pro Thermostat – General FAQ
--------------------------------------

What heating systems does SmartHeat Pro support?
SmartHeat Pro supports most 24V HVAC systems, including gas, oil, electric, and heat pumps. It does not support high-voltage systems such as 110/220V baseboard heaters.

Does SmartHeat Pro require a C-wire?
Yes, a C-wire is recommended for continuous power. If your system lacks a C-wire, you may use the included Power Adapter Kit or PEK terminal.

Can I use SmartHeat Pro without the mobile app?
Basic thermostat functions are available without the app, but remote control, scheduling, and energy reports require the SmartHeat app.

Can multiple users control the same thermostat?
Yes. You may invite additional users through the mobile app.

Can SmartHeat Pro function without Wi-Fi?
The thermostat will continue controlling heating and cooling based on your last set temperature, but advanced features such as scheduling and automations will be unavailable.

How often does the thermostat update firmware?
Firmware updates are released several times per year and download automatically when the device is idle.

What is the temperature accuracy?
SmartHeat Pro maintains temperature within ±0.5°C under normal conditions.

Does it support multiple zones?
Yes, multiple thermostats can be paired to create a multi-zone heating and cooling system.

Are software updates free?
Yes, all thermostat software and mobile app updates are free.

What are the warranty terms?
SmartHeat Pro includes a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Damage caused by incorrect wiring or electrical faults is not covered.
